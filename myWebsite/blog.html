This critical analysis piece aims to discuss the concept proposed by McLuhan which is entitled, “The medium is the message”. The basic idea of the term is that the manner in which the message is being relayed is more important than the content itself. The manner (medium) in which the information is being sent and received by the viewer has more importance than the message. There are however some controversial issues that are incongruent with the concept that McLuhan has proposed. Some examples in his discussion may appear extreme. In this critical analysis the key ideas will be discussed and analysed.

One example that McLuhan discusses can be used to support his concept. Cubism shows us how the use of abstract elements can be used to form artworks. It is this form of artwork that allows the viewer to see the perspective of the artist through the medium and not just the content. “Cubism, by seizing on instant sensory awareness, suddenly announced that the medium is the message”.

In his discussion about the concept, he uses the lightbulb as the medium. The lightbulb itself provides no content or information. The medium, which is the lightbulb, provides an experience which would not be there if the lightbulb was not present. Without the lightbulb there would be darkness. The lightbulb provides light which in essence will provide an experience that would not have been there and provides a platform for many things. Thus, the lightbulb itself which is the medium is the actual message. “A lightbulb creates an environment by its mere presence”.

Controversially, he discusses the idea of the content of television broadcasts not having an impact on society. In essence what kids are watching will have an impact on them. If a child chooses to watch violent programs, he will engage with it and possibly become a violent person. If a child chooses to watch kids’ programs, he will not pick up the habits of violence. Television will have a different effect on each child depending on what they watch.

From a Digital Arts perspective it is very interesting to relate to. During the time of McLuhan, the internet was something of the future, yet his philosophical conclusion would prove to be correct. The internet, social media and media in general are the medium to such an extent and he hypothesized that. During McLuhan’s time the media was not that extensive as we see today. The internet and social media all form different forms of mediums. The medium itself has an effect and has a role to play in society. The medium takes precedent over the content/information.

As a future software engineer or web developer, the role that I play is important in not being negligent and taking for granted the mediums that I can use in my profession. It allows for a deeper understanding when a client comes to me for an opportunity. Content will not only be the main element, taking into consideration the different mediums and platforms available can be an advantage.
