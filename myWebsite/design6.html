Overall Design
The overall design of the webpage was divided into two or three sections. This was to keep the viewer engaged within the webpages and to overcrowd too much information to the viewer at once. The homepage consisted of three main sections. The first section was very simple and minimalist but grabbed the viewers attention with the large, bold font and colour. This section would fill the centre of the screen and would be important to catch the viewers attention and draw them to want to explore more. The second section contains more information about who the website is about and a graphic image of them. The third section would allow them to explore more due to the aesthetic nature of the navigation system. The other webpages contained two sections. The first section allowing the viewer a contents list to other webpages that they may find interest in or provide personal information. The second section expanded on the topics chosen by the viewer in the first section. By using a contents list to help the viewer quickly locate a topic that interests them, allowed for the viewer not scroll through long and tedious webpages to find what they are looking for. This method provided a more efficient way of keeping the viewer engaged and not lose interest.
