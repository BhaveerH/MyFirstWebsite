Iconography
Icons offer aesthetic purpose within a webpage; however, their value goes beyond that. The icons in this webpage were used to make the website look aesthetically pleasing however they also allowed for navigation to be optimized. Viewers often easily identify and understand icons., hence they were included in the layout of this webpage. They also provide an alternate way of communication and accessibility for the viewer. The icons used in these webpages also match the design and continue the flow of the page. The icons also change colour when the viewer hovers over them, this provides feedback to the user. Backgrounds
The main background of the webpages contains an array of colours. Since the basic design of the body of the page is simple, the prominent background adds elegance and invites the viewers to explore more. This abstract background is compared to stacked Lego bricks (left image). The intent was to create an environment that was vibrant and fresh. The colours used remind one of those from comic books. Comic books may have been around for generations, but they are still popular and remain a classic. The backgrounds used for the other sections were plain or lightly patterned (right image). This was to allow for more focus on the content they were displaying. On the home page, the background of the first section is a doodle (centre image), this background has a comic book feel and expression which further enhances and complements the style.
